Start Here

Your first steps in understanding the CYFSA process and preparing for the first 45 days.

What This Roadmap Is

This guide explains the early stages of a child protection case under the CYFSA. It helps you understand what to expect, what to do, and how to stay organized.

What You Should Do First

  • Read the Days 0–7 page
  • Contact a lawyer or duty counsel
  • Request disclosure from CAS
  • Start documenting everything
  • Stay calm and avoid emotional communication

How to Use This Guide

Each page covers a specific part of the first 45 days. Follow the steps in order and use the checklists to stay organized.
