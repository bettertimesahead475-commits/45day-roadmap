Days 0–7

The first week sets the foundation for your entire case. What you do here matters.

1. Contact a Lawyer Immediately

Whether through Legal Aid, duty counsel, or private counsel, getting legal advice early is critical.

2. Request Disclosure

Ask CAS or your lawyer for the evidence being used in your case. This includes notes, reports, and allegations.

3. Start Documenting Everything

  • Dates and times of CAS contact
  • What was said
  • Any concerns or corrections
  • Access visits (if applicable)

4. Attend All Scheduled Meetings

Missing meetings early in the process can harm your credibility. Show up, be respectful, and stay calm.

5. Avoid Emotional Communication

Do not send angry or emotional messages to CAS. Keep everything brief, respectful, and written.
